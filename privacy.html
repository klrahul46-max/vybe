Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Vybe does not require account creation. We collect minimal data to provide the service. The scenario descriptions and mood inputs you provide are sent to Anthropic Claude API to generate recommendations and are not stored on our servers.

2. Local Storage

Vybe stores the following data locally on your device using browser localStorage: your vybe history (last 20 generations), usage count, streak data, and language preference. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by Razorpay (for Indian users) and Paddle (for international users). We do not store your payment card details. Please refer to Razorpay and Paddle privacy policies for how they handle payment data.

4. Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the service. This includes page visits and feature usage but does not include your personal mood inputs or scenario descriptions.

5. Third Party Services

We use Anthropic Claude API for AI generation. Your scenario inputs are processed by Anthropic subject to their privacy policy. We use ipapi.co for country detection to show localised pricing. No personally identifiable information is shared.

6. Cookies

Vybe uses localStorage rather than cookies. No tracking cookies are used.

7. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing to delete from our end. Your local vybe history can be cleared at any time using the clear history button in the app.

8. Children

Vybe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at getvybed.vercel.app
